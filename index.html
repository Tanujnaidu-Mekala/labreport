Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a lost item?
To report a lost item, click on the "Report Lost" button in the navigation menu. Fill out the form with as many details as possible about your item, including when and where you last saw it, and any identifying features.
Is there a fee for using Lost&Found?
No, our basic service is completely free. We believe in helping people reconnect with their belongings without any barriers. We do offer premium features for a small subscription fee that provides additional benefits like priority listings.
How are my personal details protected?
We take privacy seriously. Your contact information is never publicly displayed. When someone finds your item, they can contact you through our secure messaging system, and you decide if you want to share additional details.
